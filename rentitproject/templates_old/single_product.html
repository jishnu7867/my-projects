About This Camera

The Canon EOS R5 is a full-frame mirrorless camera that redefines versatility and performance. With its groundbreaking 45MP sensor, advanced autofocus system, and 8K video capability, it's the perfect tool for professional photographers and videographers.

Key Features

  • 45MP Full-Frame CMOS Sensor
  • 8K RAW Video Recording
  • Dual Pixel CMOS AF II with 1053 AF points
  • Up to 20 fps electronic shutter
  • 5-axis in-body image stabilization
  • Weather-sealed magnesium alloy body
